Front-End web developer | Graphic designer
------------------------------------------

From drawing on paper as a kid to bringing captivating visuals to life and building seamless user experiences, I just started college when COVID hit, and since there was a worldwide lockdown, every school and university here in Morocco was closed. This is where I saw the huge opportunity that I had back then and started diving into programming. Front-end web development piqued my interest after the discovery phase, where I learned data analysis, Android development, and full-stack development. Throughout this journey, I learned some programming languages in addition to the main front-end ones, such as MySQL, PHP, and Java. The knowledge that I acquired over 6 months allowed me to start my freelancing activity and started improving and honing my skills in both web development and graphic design. I have more than 3 years of experience working with both companies and individuals, and I'm still learning new technologies to stay up-to-date with what the job market needs.
